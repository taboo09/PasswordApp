Password App

Is a Web Application designed to store different types of passwords (including PIN cards, codes or other secrets).
Some of the features:
  • The Passwords and Entry Codes get encrypted before are saved in database.
  • The app has Login / Register forms.
  • Supports unlimited nr of passwords per user.
  • Auto Log Out after 30 min of LoggedIn time.
  • Using the lastest tehnologies, the app is developed in ASP.NET and ANGULAR.
  • User friendly, easy to use and intuitive.
  • And not the last...it provides a high level of safety for your Passwords.
